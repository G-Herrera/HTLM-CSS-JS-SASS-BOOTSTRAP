Portfolio Introduction

On these pages, you will find a showcase of my work as a developer, 3D artist, and digital environment designer. This portfolio includes everything from individual props with technical detail and realism, to full environments designed to create immersive atmospheres coherent with a video game's narrative.

My focus is on developing visual assets optimized for real-time engines, balancing aesthetics with functionality. Each project reflects a process that goes from modeling and retopology, to texturing and integration into interactive environments.

I invite you to explore this portfolio and discover each project crafted to enhance the player’s experience.

AT-99 SCORPIO

This project involved the detailed modeling and texturing of the AT-99 Scorpio, a futuristic vehicle designed for high-speed travel in a sci-fi setting. The goal was to create a realistic 3D representation suitable for various applications, including marketing and virtual showcases.

Crockery. Kitchen Props in 3D

This project reflects not only the work in 3D modeling and PBR texturing, but also the intention to integrate individual objects into a narrative and functional scene. The goal was to always think of their application within a game engine or as product visualization assets.

Nintendo Switch Project

This project involved the detailed modeling and texturing of the Nintendo Switch console, capturing its iconic design and features. The goal was to create a realistic 3D representation suitable for various applications, including marketing and virtual showcases.

Viking environment

This project involved the creation of a detailed Viking environment, complete with realistic lighting and atmospheric effects. The goal was to immerse viewers in a rich, historical setting that showcases the beauty and complexity of Viking architecture and landscapes.
